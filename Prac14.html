ERASER

An eraser, also known as a rubber in some countries, is a tool used to remove marks made by pencils or other writing instruments. Erasers are typically made of materials like rubber, synthetic rubber, or plastic, which have the ability to "rub off" graphite or ink when pressure is applied.
